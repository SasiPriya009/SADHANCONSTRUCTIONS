Comprehensive Electrical Infrastructure Services

We provide end-to-end solutions for high voltage transmission, distribution, and industrial power requirements, ensuring safety, quality, and timely delivery.

Expert Execution of EHT & HT Transmission Lines and Substations

Sadan Constructions specializes in the turnkey execution of Extra High Tension (EHT) and High Tension (HT) transmission lines. Our expertise covers the entire spectrum from route survey and soil investigation to tower erection and conductor stringing. We ensure that every project meets the rigorous standards required for grid stability and reliability.

  • Detailed route surveys and Right of Way (RoW) management.
  • Foundation casting, tower erection, and stringing of conductors.
  • Installation and commissioning of power transformers and switchgear.

We adhere to strict safety standards and quality control measures during every phase of construction. Our team ensures compliance with all regulatory norms, including CEIG approvals, to facilitate seamless grid synchronization.

Whether it is a greenfield project or an upgrade of existing infrastructure, we deliver robust power solutions tailored to meet the specific needs of utilities and industrial clients. Our commitment to excellence ensures that our electrical constructions stand the test of time.
