    Our Story: Powering Progress

    Since 2011

    Since our inception in 2011, we have transformed from a specialized team into a premier force in EHT & HT infrastructure. We specialize in the design, construction, and commissioning of critical power systems that drive industrial growth. Technical Expertise With a proven track record in high-stakes environments, we deliver:

    • EHT Lines: 220kV and 132kV transmission systems.
    • HT Networks: 33kV and 11kV distribution lines and substations.
    • Industrial Power: Comprehensive HT & LT power system solutions.

    We are proud partners to India’s leading utilities, maintaining a legacy of excellence with APTRANSCO, TSTRANSCO, TGTRANSCO, and all major distribution companies including TSSPDCL, TSNPDCL, and TGSPDCL
